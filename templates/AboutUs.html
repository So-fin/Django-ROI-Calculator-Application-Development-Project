Motive

Our company ROI measures the gain or loss generated on an investment relative to the amount of money invested .This is typically used for personal financial decisions, to compare a company's profitability or to compare the efficiency of different investments.

Purpose

In business, the purpose of the return on investment (ROI) company is to measure, per period, rates of return on money invested in an economic entity in order to decide whether or not to undertake an investment.

Benefits

It helps the investors and the financial professional to quickly check the prospect of an investment and thus no time and money get wasted. It assists in understanding and measuring the benefits of investment in particular departments as well. It helps to measure the competition around in the market. It also helps in exploring as well as measuring the potential returns on different investment opportunities.
